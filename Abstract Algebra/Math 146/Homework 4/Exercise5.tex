\subsection*{Exercise 4}
\textit{Give a multiplication table for the ring $\mathbb{Z}_3[x]/\langle x^2 - 1 \rangle$.}

\vspace{5 mm}
$\mathbb{Z}_3[x]/\langle x^2 - 1 \rangle = \{[0],[1],[2],[x],[x+1],[x+2],[2x],[2x+1]\}$, where [$x^2$] = [1].
